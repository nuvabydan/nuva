NUVO was founded by dansjpg with a bold mission: to redefine photography forever. The idea was simple but powerful—create a space where photographers, creatives, and visual storytellers could find inspiration, practical guidance, and a platform to showcase their work. It’s not just another blog or magazine—it’s both, combining the storytelling depth of a blog with the polished, curated experience of a magazine. Think of it as a Photographer’s 101 guide—whether you’re just starting out or already seasoned, NUVO gives you the tools, tips, and creative insights to level up your craft. From striking visuals to in-depth stories behind each shot, it’s a hub where creativity meets education, and where photographers can stay connected and inspired. Basically, NUVO isn’t just about looking at photos—it’s about understanding, learning, and pushing the boundaries of photography.
